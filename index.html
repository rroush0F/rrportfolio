About Me

Introduction:

Hello! My name is Riley Roush and I am a 27 year old who was born and raised in Indianapolis, Indiana! I love to travel often and exercise on a daily basis. I grew up in a family of racing fans and construction workers, so naturally that is where my career started. After quickly running out of money racing in Indiana, I moved to Florida and joined the family construction company. Eventually I was mentally and physically exhausted from the the manual labor and moved home to Indiana to pursue a career in coding.

My Education:

Beginning in September of 2021, I attended Eleven Fifty Academy, taking their online Web Development 12 week course. I graduated with all 3 badges in December of the same year. During my time there, I became fluent in CSS, HTML, Javascript, SQL, and ReactJS. I also learned how to efficiently use Bootstrap, Firebase, Github, and other extremely helpful services. In the next section, I will be including project examples of what I was able to accomplish during the course.
